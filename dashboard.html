Pro Tips

Keyboard Shortcuts: Use Ctrl+B for bold, Ctrl+I for italic, and Ctrl+U for underline in notes.
Color Coding: Use different colors for your notes to organize topics and highlight important information.
Search: Use the search function in notes to quickly find specific information.
Mobile Friendly: Access your flashcards on any device - the interface adapts to your screen size.
